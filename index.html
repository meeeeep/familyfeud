Rules of the Game

  • Be the first to reach up to 300.
  • Get all of the answers correct on the board and you will recieve all of the points generated from that round.
  • Like the game show, you have 3 chances to submit incorrect answers.
  • If you submit 3 incorrect answers, you loose your turn and the points to the other family
  • Have fun, and enjoy!
