Rules of the Game

  • Be the first to reach up to 300 points in 4 rounds.
  • Get all of the answers correct on the board and you will recieve all of the points generated from that round.
  • Like the game show, you have 3 chances to submit incorrect answers.
  • If you submit 3 incorrect answers, you will loose all the points in that round to the computer.
  • Also, each round is timed. You will have 120 seconds to type and submit the correct answers.
  • If you run out of time, you will only be given the points you earned from each submitted correct answer.
  • Have fun, and enjoy!
